Step 3

3D-Printed Floating Plant System

Revolutionary architecture for triple-output generation

Our innovative system features Pistia stratiotes (Laitue d'eau) plants floating in a specially designed 3D-printed pot with optimized layer architecture. This groundbreaking setup combines phytoremediation with Plant Microbial Fuel Cell (PMFC) technology while promoting accelerated plant growth through controlled nutrient uptake.

Multi-Layer Architecture

Soil layer → Graphite anode layer → Second soil layer → Wastewater layer → Floating plant with partially immersed cathode exposed to air.

Enhanced Phytoremediation

The floating Pistia stratiotes (Laitue d'eau) plant efficiently filters pollutants through its extensive root system while accessing optimal nutrients for accelerated growth.

Optimized Electricity Generation

Bacteria in the graphite layer break down organic matter, generating electrons captured by the buried anode and transferred to the surface cathode.

Accelerated Plant Growth

The controlled floating environment provides optimal nutrient access, resulting in faster growth rates and increased biomass production.
